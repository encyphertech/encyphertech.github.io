Welcome to enCypher

enCypher Technologies Private Limited is an organisation founded by the alumni’s of NIEC Delhi of IP University and the founders of the ROBOGYAN society. Keen-to-learn engineers comprise our core team.

Our key to success is learning by doing. We see ourselves as the only competitor in the market to compete. Focussed on cost effective and better work quality, we, encyphers, provide solutions in the fields of Internet of Things (IoT), alternatively known as M2M, Tele-matics, Tele-presence robotics, Content security, Web, Mobile and Cloud based design and development.

Our Products and Solutions are based on System Integration and Networking. It covers all the aspects from Hardware integration to Cross Platform Application Development. We are specialized in Embedded Systems, Advance Robotics Products, Real-time Advance Communication System for Robots and Devices or Control over Cloud (COC). Web Designing, SEO, app development are the other areas of services. We have an experience in respective fields. The enterprise strives to dispense the technological solutions to its clients.

enCyphers : Our journey..

Working alone in different backgrounds, we have had experiences in different technologies. We share the thought of learning by sharing. So, to share, we formed the first ever technological society of our college and this marked the first milestone of our journey.

Then, moving forward, we organised a few DIY workshops and provided students with hand-on-experience. With huge positive response and increased enthusiasm we thought of taking this to a larger scale.

So, here we stand in the market to deliver what is required, accomplish what is demanded, share what is felt, learn what is new and grow with a sense of taking India to a new level.

Who is enCypher

we will deliver IoT end-to-end products and solutions. Our starting solutions will rely on short-haul communication standards that are not proprietary to any organization, firm or government body. Later on we will move to cellular integration

we will provide a middleware for IoT applications and products for rapid development and fast deployment. Middleware services provide a more functional set of application programming interfaces (APIs) to provide services beyond those provided by the operating system thus providing interaction with another service or application

we are on our way to create Delhi first IoT lab to shape new ideas into reality regarding this field and will make people aware of the new upcoming market and technologies trends by the means of workshops

our plan is to become a regional leader in IoT and M2M Solutions, software, web, mobile and cloud based design and development; publishing; electronics manufacturing, hardware & networking, training, research and development
